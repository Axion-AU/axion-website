# Axion Ventures Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence

**One line:** Every venture starts with something broken.

**Core identity:** Axion is the strategic and technical backbone behind data-driven ventures. It's a workshop, not a showroom. The brand should feel like the place where serious products get engineered, not the place where they get pitched.

## Name

**Axion** is a hypothetical subatomic particle in physics, proposed to solve the strong CP problem. The name carries connotations of something fundamental, structural, and foundational, a particle that makes the universe work even though you can't see it directly.

It also sounds like "action" and "axis," both of which map onto the studio's function: the axis around which ventures rotate, the action of building.

The name is deliberately not flashy. It's not a verb, not a metaphor, not a pun. It's a noun that sounds like it belongs in a physics journal. That's the register: serious, structural, precise.

## Brand Positioning

### What Axion Is

A solo-founder venture studio powered by SPARC. One person building data-driven products where AI does the actual processing. The studio consolidates IP, methodology, and design patterns across ventures.

### What Axion Is Not

- A fund (it doesn't invest in other companies)
- An accelerator (it doesn't take in cohorts)
- A consultancy (it doesn't do client work)
- A large team pretending to be lean (it's one person, and the brand should be honest about that)

### Audience

Axion's brand serves three audiences, none of which are end users of its ventures:

1. **Potential investors/partners.** People evaluating whether to fund Nexus or collaborate on future ventures. They need to see competence, track record, and honest status.
2. **Other founders and builders.** People who might use SPARC, learn from Axion's approach, or become future collaborators. They need to see the methodology and the thinking.
3. **Ethan's professional network.** The brand is also a portfolio. It should make the work legible to anyone evaluating Ethan as a technologist and founder.

End users of Zetto see "An Axion Ventures project" in the footer. End users of Nexus see the same. They don't need to engage with the Axion brand at all.

## Visual Identity

### Design Philosophy: Refined Industrial

The aesthetic borrows from two traditions:

1. **Editorial design.** The clarity and confidence of a well-set research paper or architecture journal. Generous white space, precise typography, information that breathes.
2. **Workshop documentation.** The functional beauty of technical specs, blueprints, and engineering logs. Data displayed cleanly, status communicated honestly, nothing decorative.

The result should feel like a document you trust. Not a landing page trying to impress you. A place where the work speaks and the design stays out of its way.

### Color Palette

**Foundation: Warm Paper**

Axion's palette is deliberately light and restrained. This contrasts with Zetto (dark, vermillion) and prevents the parent brand from competing visually with its ventures.

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Warm Linen | `#F8F6F3` | Primary background |
| Surface | White | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, raised elements |
| Inset | Stone Wash | `#EFECE8` | Recessed areas, code blocks, callouts |
| Border | Rule | `rgba(28, 25, 23, 0.08)` | Dividers, card borders |

**Ink: Warm Neutrals**

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary text | Ink | `#1C1917` | Headlines, body text |
| Secondary text | Ink Mid | `#44403C` | Body text, descriptions |
| Tertiary text | Ink Light | `#78716C` | Captions, labels, metadata |
| Faint text | Ink Faint | `#A8A29E` | Timestamps, disabled states |

**Accent: Signal Blue**

A single accent color used sparingly for interactive elements, status indicators, and emphasis.

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Accent | Signal Blue | `#2563EB` | Links, active status, key data points |
| Accent dim | Blue Wash | `rgba(37, 99, 235, 0.08)` | Status badge backgrounds |
| Accent mid | Blue Tint | `rgba(37, 99, 235, 0.15)` | Text selection, hover states |

**Color rules:**
- Blue is the only non-neutral color on the Axion site. No gradients, no secondary accents.
- Venture cards can show a hint of their own brand color (Zetto's vermillion, Nexus's palette) in status badges or icons only. Never enough to clash with Axion's palette.
- The warm paper background is not white. Pure white (#FFFFFF) is reserved for raised surfaces (cards, panels). The background has warmth (#F8F6F3) that prevents the clinical feel of a blank screen.

### Typography

**Display / Headlines: Instrument Serif**

A contemporary serif with personality. The italic weight has a distinctive calligraphic quality that gives headlines elegance without formality. Used for all headings, the hero statement, and the footer tagline.

| Use | Weight | Size Range |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| Hero headline | Regular (400) | 40-64px |
| Section headlines | Regular (400) | 28-40px |
| Venture names | Regular (400) | 24-28px |
| Footer tagline | Italic (400i) | 16px |

**Body: DM Sans**

Clean, geometric, highly legible. Light weight (300) for body text gives an airy, editorial feel. Regular and medium weights for emphasis.

| Use | Weight | Size Range |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| Body text | Light (300) | 16-18px |
| Emphasis | Regular (400) | 16-18px |
| Strong emphasis | Medium (500) | 16-18px |

**Data / Labels: DM Mono**

Monospace for anything that represents data, status, metadata, or technical information. This signals "this is measured" vs "this is written."

| Use | Weight | Size Range |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| Section labels | Medium (500) | 10-11px, uppercase, tracked |
| Status badges | Regular (400) | 10-11px, uppercase |
| Data points | Regular (400) | 13px |
| Legal / footer meta | Regular (400) | 11px |
| Code / technical | Regular (400) | 13-14px |

**Type rules:**
- Headlines are always Instrument Serif. Never set a headline in DM Sans.
- Body text is always DM Sans Light (300). Regular (400) only for emphasis within body copy.
- Anything that could appear in a spreadsheet or a terminal uses DM Mono.
- Letter-spacing on mono labels: 0.08-0.12em. Tight enough to read, open enough to signal "label."
- No all-caps except in DM Mono labels. Headlines and body text are always sentence case.

### Logo

**The mark:** A square border containing an italic serif "A" (Instrument Serif italic). A small accent-colored corner bracket at the bottom-right corner of the square.

**Construction:**
- Square: 1.5px stroke, ink color (#1C1917)
- "A": Instrument Serif italic, centered, sized to ~56% of the square height
- Corner bracket: 1.5px stroke, accent blue (#2563EB), covering ~25% of the bottom-right corner edges

**Why this works:**
- The italic "A" references the mathematical/physics notation origin of "axion"
- The square containment echoes Zetto's boxed ゼット mark (connected DNA without matching)
- The blue corner bracket is the only accent color in the mark, drawing the eye to the bottom-right (forward, down: building, grounding)
- At small sizes (favicon, 16px), the mark simplifies to just the square with the blue corner bracket (the "A" can be dropped)

**Wordmark:** "AXION VENTURES" in DM Mono Medium, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.08em. The wordmark always accompanies the mark except when the mark appears as a favicon or app icon.

**Logo variants:**
- Full: Mark + wordmark (horizontal lockup)
- Compact: Mark only (app icon, favicon, social avatar)
- Text only: Wordmark without mark (footer, inline references)

**Clear space:** Minimum clear space around the full logo is 1x the height of the mark on all sides.

**Backgrounds:** The mark works on both light (default) and dark backgrounds. On dark backgrounds, the square stroke and "A" become warm white (#E8E4DF), the blue corner bracket stays blue.

### Iconography

Minimal. Axion's site barely needs icons. Where they appear:

- Status indicators: small colored dots (blue for active, stone for on hold) next to DM Mono text labels. No icon shapes.
- Arrows: simple → characters in DM Mono for links and navigation. No SVG arrow icons.
- External links: append " →" to link text. No external-link icons.

If future expansion requires icons, they should be 1.5px stroke, rounded caps, geometric, and monochrome (ink color only, never blue unless interactive).

### Motion

**Restrained.** Axion's motion is barely perceptible. The site should feel like a well-typeset document that happens to be interactive, not an animated experience.

- Page load: Content fades up with 0.6s ease-out, staggered by 0.08s per element. Subtle enough that most users won't consciously notice.
- Hover states: Color transitions only, 0.2s duration. No scale, no shadows, no transforms.
- Card hover: Border color darkens slightly. Nothing else.
- No scroll-triggered animations beyond the initial reveal. Once an element is visible, it stays static.

### Photography and Illustration

Axion does not use photography or illustration on its site. The content is text, data, and structure. If photography is ever needed for marketing materials, follow these rules:

- Workspace photography only: screens, notebooks, whiteboards, code. Never portraits, never stock photos of people shaking hands or looking at post-it notes.
- Desaturated, warm tone to match the linen palette.
- No AI-generated imagery. Ever.

### Relationship to Venture Brands

Axion's visual identity is the quiet backdrop. Each venture has its own brand that takes priority in user-facing contexts.

| Element | Axion | Zetto | Nexus |
|---------|-------|-------|-------|
| Mood | Editorial, restrained | Dark, precise, confrontational | Warm, collaborative (TBD) |
| Background | Warm linen | Sumi black | TBD |
| Accent | Signal blue | Vermillion | TBD |
| Typography | Serif headlines + sans body | Noto Sans JP + IBM Plex Mono | TBD |
| Logo shape | Square with italic A | Square with ゼット | TBD |
| Tone | Confident, quiet | Confident, provocative | Empathetic, direct |

The shared DNA: both Axion and Zetto use a square-contained logo mark with a colored corner accent. This is the only visual thread connecting them. Everything else is deliberately different so the ventures stand on their own.

## Voice and Tone

### Brand Voice

Defined in full in the Axion Messaging Guidelines. Summary:

- **Direct.** Say what Axion is and isn't.
- **Specific.** Name the ventures. Show the work. Use numbers.
- **Honest about stage.** Pre-launch is pre-launch. On hold is on hold.
- **First person for the founder.** "I'm Ethan." Not "Ethan Cornwill is a systems thinker."

### Words Axion Uses

- Build, ship, design, engineer
- Data-driven, adaptive, measured
- Venture, studio, methodology
- Honest, specific, concrete

### Words Axion Never Uses

- Disrupt, revolutionize, transform
- World-class, cutting-edge, next-generation
- Passionate, innovative, visionary
- Ecosystem, synergy, leverage
- Any sentence that could describe 500 other companies without changing a word
