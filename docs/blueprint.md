# **App Name**: Axion Ventures

## Core Features:

- Home Page: Display a hero section with tagline, company introduction, and CTAs to explore ventures and collaboration.
- About Page: Present Axion's mission, concept of iterative learning, strategic evolution, and the founder's vision with optional tech-themed imagery.
- Ventures Showcase: Display current and past ventures (SPARC, Nexus, etc.) with descriptions and 'Learn More' CTAs.
- Collaboration Invitation: Invite potential collaborators via a form to capture their Name, Email, Interest/Expertise, and Message.
- Team Introduction: Introduce Axion's founder, their philosophy, and leave placeholders for future team members.
- Contact Form: Provide a contact form for user inquiries with fields for Name, Email, and Message, along with contact details and social media links.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#4B0082), embodying sophistication and innovation. 
- Background color: Dark blue (#191970), desaturated to 20% and darkened for contrast and professionalism.
- Accent color: Metallic gold (#B8860B), analogous to purple, to provide highlights on interactive elements and reinforce the high-end feel.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif), providing a modern, geometric aesthetic. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), suitable for long text.
- Responsive design ensures seamless adaptation across devices, with smooth scrolling for enhanced user experience.
- Abstract geometric icons inspired by network motifs underscore innovation while maintaining a minimalist look.
- Subtle hover effects and interactive states on buttons and forms improve usability.
